IPEvents Story

Irelands best Concession hire company

IPEvents is a branch of Irish popcorn and snackfood company that mainly focuses on the machine / event hire side of the business. Established in 1995 the Irish Popcorn & Snackfood Company are renowned specialists within the funfood industry for the supply and maintenance of funfood equipment and products in Ireland, the UK, and Europe. Our ‘Equipment & Supply Products’ include Popcorn, Nachos, Ice Cream, Candyfloss, Hotdog’s, Slush, Hot Nuts, Crepes, Waffles, Pretzels, Candy Apples, Paper Products, Branded Goods, and Much, Much More! We supply all machines, raw material and finished products to outlets all over Ireland, the UK, and Europe. We install, service and guarantee every machine that we supply. Irish popcorn has been hiring out machines for events since 2010 but had never really put resources into it untill 2019 when after a surge in event hire it was seen as a lucrative part of the business. We decided to seperate the section of the business and dedicate more time and resources to event hire as a whole. IPEvents is dedicated to providing the best service paired with the highest quality machines in the industry.
